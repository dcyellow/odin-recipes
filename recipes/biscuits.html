Buscuits

This three-ingredient recipe gets biscuits on your table in a quick and easy manner for when you need biscuits now!

Ingredients:

  • 2 1/4 cups self-rising flour
  • 3/4 cup shortening
  • 1 cup milk

Steps:

  1. Combine and mix ingredients together, and pour out on floured waxed paper
  2. Pat the dough out with your hands until dough is not sticky (add flour if necessary)
  3. Fold double
  4. Cut buscuits with a buscuit cutter
  5. Bake on a cookie sheet at 425 degrees F (220 degrees C) for 20-25 minutes
  6. Enjoy!
