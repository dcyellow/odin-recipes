Banana Bread

This easy banana bread recipe is quick to prepare, and everyone always comes back for seconds!

Ingredients:

  • 3 ripe bananas, mashed
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup melted butter
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C)
  2. Grease a 9x5-inch loaf pan
  3. Combine bananas, sugar, egg, and butter together in a bowl. Mix flour and bakking soda together in a seperate bowl
  4. Stir into banana mixture until batter is just mixed.
  5. Stir salt into better
  6. Pour batter into the prepared loaf pan
  7. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted in the center comes back clean, usually 1 hour
  8. Enjoy!
